# Notora – Notes Organizer

Notora – Notes Organizer is a fast, offline-first notes app designed for students who want a clean, distraction-free workspace. Think minimalist like Obsidian, but fully in your browser—no servers, no accounts, everything stays on your device.

---

## What Notora Does

* **Create Notes Quickly:** Add, edit, and organize your notes with instant auto-save.
* **Attach Anything:** Upload images, PDFs, MP3s, or DOCX files and see them directly in your notes.
* **Image & PDF Previews:** Images appear as thumbnails you can click to enlarge; PDFs are embedded for easy reading.
* **Link Previews:** Paste a URL, and if you’re online, a mini preview appears automatically.
* **Export & Share:** Save notes as text files or share them as images for easy sharing.
* **Minimalist & Stylish:** Rounded buttons, smooth transitions, collapsible sidebar, and a clean light mode interface.

---

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox recommended).
2. Click “+ New Note” to start writing.
3. Upload attachments using the file input below the note.
4. Use the toolbar buttons to export notes as text or images.
5. Collapse the sidebar to give more space when writing.

Everything is stored locally—no internet required. Just don’t clear your browser data!

---

## Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* **Storage:** IndexedDB (notes and files stay in your browser)
* **Extras:** `html2canvas` for exporting notes as images

---

## Why Notora?

You don’t need a slow, bloated cloud app to take notes. Notora – Notes Organizer is **fast, simple, and offline-ready**, perfect for students who want a clean, organized space for all their study materials.
